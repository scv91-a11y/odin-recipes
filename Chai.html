How to make authentic Masala Chai!

The Recipe

Making chai isn’t tricky, it just requires a handful of spices and a bit of patience. Here’s the step-by-step tutorial.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 4 black tea bags
  • 2-3 cardamom, cloves and vanilla
  • 1 inch thinly sliced ginger

Preparation

  1. Step One: Combine thinly sliced fresh ginger, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, and vanilla, along with honey and sugar in 2 cups of water. Bring to a boil and then simmer for five minutes.
  2. Step Two: Remove from heat, add four black tea bags, and steep for 10 minutes.
  3. Step Three: Scoop out the tea bags, ginger slices, and cinnamon stick.
  4. Step Four : Add two cups of milk. I prefer whole or two percent milk here, since a little richness goes a long way. You can also use plant milk. My preferred one is a blend of almond and coconut milk.
  5. Step Five: Gently heat the tea to your liking, pour into mugs, and savor every sip.
